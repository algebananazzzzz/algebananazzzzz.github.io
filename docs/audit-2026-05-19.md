## Findings

The initial audit (pre-fix) revealed three categories of issues across all five routes. First, all muted-label text used `text-gray-500` (#6a7282) against the `#0f172a` night background, yielding a contrast ratio of 3.69:1 — below WCAG AA's 4.5:1 threshold; this was fixed site-wide by promoting all muted labels to `text-gray-400` (#9ca3af, ~7.5:1). Second, heading order was violated on `/experience`, `/projects`, and `/milky-way` because `h3` card headings appeared directly under a page `h1` with no intervening `h2`; ExperienceRow and ProjectCard were updated to use `h2`, and non-structural label elements in MilkyWay and NotePanel were changed to `p`. Third, the Splash island produced React hydration errors (#418/#423/#425) because `computeStardate()` computed a different timestamp server-side versus client-side, and the button `aria-label` ("descend") did not match its visible text ("descent"); `suppressHydrationWarning` was added to the stardate span and the aria-label was corrected. After fixes, all five desktop routes reached 100/100/100/100 and both mobile routes reached 100 A11y / 100 BP / 100 SEO.
